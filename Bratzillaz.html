Our History

After the popularity of Monster High blew up, MGA followed a long established tradition of making their own version of their rivals dolls. They came up with Bratzillaz, the witchy cousins of the Bratz.

While loved by many fans of the gothic and edgy when released, it did not last as long as their rival Monster High. Bratzillaz has since gained a cult following after their released and while MGA's social media has brought them up since,

It is very unlikely they will be rebooted or rereleased due to the current generation of Monster High's including witches in their new lore.
