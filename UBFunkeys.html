U.B. Funkeys was produced by Mattel and it was released in 2007. They are stylized figurines and one of the earliest known 'Toy to Life' games, where the figurines have a chip that plugged into a larger figurine

to allow the user to enter something called the 'hub' to explore the world 'Terrapinia'.
