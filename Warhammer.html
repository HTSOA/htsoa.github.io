Warhammer is a series based on miniatures that consist of robots, goblins, orcs, humans, etc, and they play war based on the directives and information gathered from their lore. There is a big community and culture

based around this game. They have gatherings to play, they have workshops so you know how to assemble and paint the minatures, as well as online communities discussing any and all aspects of the game.
